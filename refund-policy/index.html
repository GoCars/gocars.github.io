Refund Policy

This refund policy is adopted by all clients of GoCabs.co Cars Ltd and will apply to all taxis/minicabs as arranged on the customers behalf through GoCabs.co Cars Ltd. Definitions PHV means Private Hire Vehicle but is used interchangeably with Taxis and means Taxis aswell. GoCars.co is a trading name of GoCabs.co Cars Ltd
App and Web Bookings: - We will send the vehicle to meet you at the main pre-defined pick up points The time you specified will be sent to the driver for reference, through the client of GoCabs.co Cars Ltd. The charge for the waiting time will be decided by clients of GoCabs.co Cars Ltd, by reference and consultation with GoCabs.co Cars Ltd, and will depend on the type of vehicle being used for the particular journey. Such queries should be made at the time of booking so as to avoid any subsequent disagreements.
Fixed price journeys must not deviate from the drivers chosen route, any deviation will result in the journey reverting to a fare decided by consultation with GoCabs.co Cars Ltd, such consultation will occur with GoCabs.co Cars Ltd and their client. GoCabs.co Cars Ltd will decide the charge with their clients according to the additional mileage and delay caused by the passenger or by the prevailing traffic conditions.
GoCabs.co Cars Ltd and their clients cannot be held responsible for any loss financial, professional, missed connection times, flights, trains due to the PHV not arriving at the pick-up address at the booked time due to adverse weather, traffic conditions, road traffic accidents or road closures or being given the wrong booking information by the customer or customer booked it online and got the booking reference but did not receive any confirmation from GoCabs.co Cars Ltd. [Customer must receive confirmation before the journey.]
Normal luggage assumes that 1 medium suitcase and 1 piece of hand luggage 56 cm (H) * 45 cm (W) * 25 cm (D) (approximately 22 inches x 17.5 inches x 9.85 inches) per person, any luggage that the PHV cannot accommodate is the responsibility of the passenger.
Any journey cancelled less than 24 hours prior to the booked time will result in a charge to the customers credit/debit card. The amount to be charged in such a situation will be decided by GoCabs.co Cars Ltd in consultation with its clients depending on the pick up distance/place/parking or other charges subject to a minimum of £25.00.
Any fouling of the PHV by any passenger will result in a charge of a minimum of £25.00, depending on the severity of the fouling.
GoCabs.co Cars Ltd (on behalf of its client and subsequent driver) will prosecute any person who causes damage to any PHV booked through or arranged through GoCabs.co Cars Ltd.
The driver has the right to refuse any passenger that he feels may be a threat to him/herself or vehicle.
