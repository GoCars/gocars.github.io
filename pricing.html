Pricing

To get an estimated price for a journey enter a start and end position on the app.
Our pricing is based on a cloud based meter system to give you the best possible prices. Our current minimum fare is £3.80 and upto £2.40 per mile. Prices may change at any time based on demand, wait time for driver and other factors.
